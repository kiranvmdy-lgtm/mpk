Story

Karan, a poor mechanic, leaves his daughter Suman with his wealthy friend Kishan while he works abroad. Suman befriends Kishan’s son Prem; after facing humiliation at a party, they realize they’re in love. Prem’s mother approves, but Kishan—manipulated by Ranjeet—rejects the match. Karan returns, clashes with Kishan, and takes Suman back to their village. Prem follows and accepts Karan’s challenge to prove he can support Suman independently. Despite sabotage by Jeevan, Prem’s determination wins Karan over. In the finale, Prem, Karan, and Kishan unite against Ranjeet and Jeevan, rescue Suman, reconcile, and Suman and Prem marry.
